# public/assets/objects/

Per-object PNG sprites: Resource Nodes, Structures, the Guardian and Realm Wardens, altars,
crystals, trophies, and world dressing.

- Many are cropped/recolored from CC0 packs or composed by `tools/compose-*.ts`; a few are
  original procedural art. Full breakdown in `CREDITS.md`.
- Regenerate composed sprites with their `tools/compose-*` script — don't hand-edit the PNG.
